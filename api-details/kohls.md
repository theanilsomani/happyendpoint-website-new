## 🛍️ Kohls Data API – Real-Time Retail & Product Information

**- Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

Welcome to the **Kohls Data API**, the most reliable and complete solution for accessing **Kohls.com data** programmatically.

Easily integrate **Kohls product data**, **pricing**, **inventory status**, **categories**, **store locations**, **product reviews**, and **customer Q&As** into your app, dashboard, or research tool.

### 💡 What You Can Do with This API

- **Product Search API** – Search and fetch detailed product information directly from Kohls.com
- **Product Reviews API** – Retrieve authentic customer reviews and ratings
- **Product Q&A API** – Get buyer questions and seller answers for better insights
- **Categories API** – Explore and map Kohls’ full product category tree
- **Stores Locator API** – Find Kohls store locations with addresses and contact details

### ⚙️ Use Cases

- Build **price comparison tools** or **retail analytics dashboards**
- Track **product availability and stock changes**
- Enrich **eCommerce datasets** or **affiliate websites**
- Conduct **market research** or **brand intelligence analysis**
