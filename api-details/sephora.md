## 📌 API Endpoints (11 Total)

### 🔍 Discovery & Search

- `/auto-complete` - Product suggestions from partial queries
- `/search-by-keyword` - Advanced keyword search with filters
- `/search-by-category` - Fetch products by category ID
- `/search-by-brand` - Retrieve products by brand

### 🗂️ Catalog & Metadata

- `/brands-list` - Full list of Sephora brands
- `/categories-list` - Root product categories
- `/category-data` - Subcategories and metadata

### 📦 Product Intelligence

- `/product-details` - Detailed product data (images, ingredients, pricing)
- `/product-reviews` - Customer reviews & ratings
- `/product-availability` - Store-level stock data

### 📍 Location Services

- `/store-list` - Nearby Sephora store locations
