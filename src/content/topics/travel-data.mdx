Travel applications need dynamic pricing data that changes by the hour. Hotel rates shift by occupancy, length of stay, booking channel, and seasonal demand. Flight prices move even faster. Static datasets are not useful for live booking products.

The Priceline Pro API gives you the real-time travel data layer your product needs, backed by Priceline's own pricing infrastructure.
