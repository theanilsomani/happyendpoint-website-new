Spain is one of Europe's most active real estate markets, driven by a large domestic market and significant international buyer demand - particularly in the Balearic Islands, Costa del Sol, and Barcelona. Fotocasa is the dominant Spanish property portal, and the Fotocasa API gives you structured access to its full listing inventory.

## Fotocasa API

The API covers 1.5M+ live property listings across Spain with granular filtering and market analytics:

**Property search** - filter by location, price range, property type (apartment, villa, penthouse, land), transaction type (sale, rent), size, beds, baths, and condition (new, second-hand, rebuild).

**Location autocomplete** - resolves to municipality, neighborhood, or urbanization level. Supports both Spanish and Catalan place names.

**Detailed listings** - each listing includes price, surface area (m2), floor, orientation, energy rating, parking, storage, listing agent, photos, and geo-coordinates.

**Market analytics** - average price per square meter by municipality or neighborhood, with trend data. Useful for area comparison and investment research.

**Real-time updates** - new listings and price changes reflect on the platform within minutes.

**RESTful JSON API** - stable field names, no proxies required.

## Spanish market coverage

Fotocasa's inventory spans every major Spanish market:

- **Barcelona and Catalonia** - apartments, penthouses, and villas in the metropolitan area and along the Costa Brava
- **Madrid** - the largest urban market in Spain, with deep inventory in the city and commuter belt
- **Valencia** - coastal and inland properties across the Valencia region
- **Malaga and Costa del Sol** - one of the highest-demand markets for international buyers
- **Balearic Islands** - Ibiza, Mallorca, and Menorca
- **Seville, Bilbao, Zaragoza** - major regional capitals

## Use cases

**Property portal** - power a Spain-focused property search with filters that match the Spanish buyer/renter journey.

**International buyer tools** - many buyers searching Spanish property are not in Spain. A product that presents Spanish listings in English with area analytics helps them evaluate without site visits.

**Price monitoring** - track asking price trends in a specific municipality over time. Useful for estate agents and investment advisors operating in Spanish markets.

**Rental yield analysis** - combine rental asking prices with sale prices in the same area to estimate gross yield. The Balearic Islands and Madrid city centre are among the most-researched markets for this use case.

**Investment screening** - filter listings by price-per-square-meter against the area market average to identify potentially undervalued properties.
