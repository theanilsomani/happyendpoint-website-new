The finance vertical in our catalog covers two distinct product types: institutional investment data for financial applications, and cross-retailer shopping intelligence for consumer-facing products.

## Investment data (Morningstar)

The Morningstar API provides institutional-grade financial data used by investment research platforms, portfolio tools, and fintech applications. It covers equities, ETFs, mutual funds, and fundamental data with ESG risk ratings.

Real-time quotes, financial statements, earnings transcripts, ownership data, and dividend history - all from one of the most trusted names in investment research.

## Shopping intelligence (Klarna)

The Klarna Ecom API is a cross-retailer product intelligence tool. It returns product data from multiple retailers simultaneously across 13 regions, with price history charts and current deal tracking. Useful for price comparison products, deal discovery apps, and consumer shopping tools.

This is Klarna's product discovery infrastructure, not Klarna's payment data.
