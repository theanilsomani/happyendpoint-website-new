The Sephora US Full Product Catalog is the most comprehensive beauty dataset we offer. It covers every active SKU on Sephora.com - from skincare serums and foundations to fragrance and hair tools - with the structured fields you need to build recommendation engines, price trackers, trend dashboards, and competitive intelligence tools.

## What's included

Each record maps to a single product SKU and includes the product name, brand, top-level category (Skincare, Makeup, Fragrance, Hair, Bath & Body, Tools), and subcategory. Pricing captures both the retail and current sale price so you can calculate discount depth over time. Ratings are expressed as a weighted average with the total review count, giving you a reliable signal of social proof.

Product images are delivered as direct CDN URLs covering the primary product shot and up to six gallery images. Where Sephora provides ingredient data, it is included as a parsed list - useful for building clean/vegan filters or allergen detection tools.

## Ideal use cases

This dataset powers beauty retail analytics platforms that track assortment changes, new launches, and price movements. It is widely used in recommendation engines that need a canonical product graph for cross-sell and upsell. Brand monitoring teams use it to track share of shelf and rating trends across the Sephora marketplace. ML teams training product classification or image recognition models also rely on this dataset for its consistent category taxonomy.

## Delivery and updates

The dataset is delivered as a single flat CSV or a line-delimited JSON file, your choice. A full refresh is produced weekly. If you need daily delta files (new products, price changes, delisted SKUs), that cadence is available on request.
